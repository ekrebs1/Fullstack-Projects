Portfolio About Home

Some passions and interests I enjoy outside of work that help me with keeping a healthy balance.

Traveling

Having a curious mind has always led me on a path to travel and explore new parts of the world. I have currently traveled to thirteen different countries and plan to visit more. Traveling to the unknown has always brought a sense of uncertainty that I enjoy because it continues to bring me out of my comfort zone. Exploring these different regions and cultures of the world will always be a passion of mine.

Fashion

Waking up everyday and having the creative freedom to dress according to your mood and/or style is a luxury I enjoy every day. Having the ability to express yourself through an outfit is one of my favorite forms of expression. There is never a right or wrong way because you set the rules. Taking risks and staying ahead of the trends are just two reasons why I love fashion.
